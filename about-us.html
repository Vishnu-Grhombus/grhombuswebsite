About Us

The Rhombus in GRhombus logo signifies that our dedication meets the customer's interest at right angles to produce a world-class and competent collaboration. Our values and customer's satisfaction go hand in hand like the parallel sides of the Rhombus.
‍
GRhombus Technologies Pvt Ltd, a pioneer in Software Solutions – Especially on Test Automation, Cyber Security, Full Stack Development, DevOps, Salesforce, Performance Testing and Manual Testing. Our business sphere started in May 2014 and is run by experts with 25+ years of experience in the industry. We are 200+ members of the GRhombus family. We provide services to our clients from different countries like USA, UK, Canada and India. We have multiple projects on hand with a highly qualified and dedicated team to handle each project, ensuring an uninterrupted flow of delivery to the customer. GRhombus is a great success story in any dimension and committed to long-term nourishing relationships with all the people around the world. GRhombus Technologies Private Limited is a subsidiary of GRhombus Consulting.

GRhombus delivery centers are located in India at Hyderabad, Chennai, Bengaluru and Pune. In Middle east, we are located in Dubai. Our partner offices are located in USA and Netherlands.
