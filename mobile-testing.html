Mobile Testing

In today’s interconnected world everything is accessible by just a click on the mobile and it has become highly unimaginable for any enterprise to run and succeed without having a Mobile Application. The rapid pace at which the technological advancements are taking place, Smartphones and the mobile applications have become a part of our livelihood and its usage has spread across business nowadays. Hence Mobile Testing plays an important role. Mobile apps are deeply connected in every venture such as E-Commerce, E-Ticketing, Taxi services, Banking, Healthcare, Entertainment, News, Education, Food and Social Media.

Mobile application testing is crucial. It involves testing across multiple devices, operating systems, networks and platforms. Typically, the process involves testing the applications for compatibility, usability and their functionalities such as device permissions, interactions, swipe, wi-fi connectivity etc. Mobile testing can be either done manually or can be automated.

Mobile Security Testing

Mobile application security testing can help ensure there aren't any loopholes in the software that may cause data loss. Here, the app is attacked in every possible way to identify potential vulnerabilities or loop holes that may be misused by an external system or persons and prevent their access to the personal information available on the mobile device.

Mobile penetration testing is a type of security test that is specifically designed to predict the behaviour of hackers to find flaws and fix them before hackers get a chance to exploit. We offers both iOS and android penetration testing by using sophisticated tools and our advanced knowledge of IT experts to guess the behaviour of an attacker and fix the problem before it gets taken advantage of.

Key Challenges:

The key aspects/challenges that needed to be tested for mobile testing can be observed as follows:

  • Checking if the application is downloadable from app stores on both android and iOS devices.

  • Accessibility over various mobile networks such as CDMA, GSM and less common ones such as FOMA, and TD-SCDMA.

  • Scripting across a variety of devices as they differ in, they keystroke and input methods

  • Compatibility tests must be done to ensure an application works well on both high-resolution and low-resolution devices alike.

  • Apps must be tested to ensure they do not block calls/ SMS on the mobile, stop other notifications or interrupt power cycle.

  • To ensure an app behaves alike in all conditions such as low battery, bad network coverage, low memory space etc. mobile performance testing must be done.

  • Security testing to check an apps vulnerability to hacking and data loss

  • Load testing to ensure how the app works if multiple users attempt to download/use the app at the same time

  • Usability testing to ensure the app performs as it is designed for and gets a favorable response from its users.
