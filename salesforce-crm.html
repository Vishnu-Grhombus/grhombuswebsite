Salesforce CRM

Business Process Study

Every business comes with its unique needs. The Salesforce CRM application. recognizes that and gives you the provision of amending itself to fit in the way of your business.

Customization

Salesforce’s functionality is utilized to its fullest by customizing existing CRM solutions to meet the business process and workflow needs of the organization in question.

Custom Application Development

Customization includes advanced automation functions or expand on the functionality of existing Salesforce CRM solution through powerful custom application development, possible through creating Visualforce pages, Apex classes, S-controls, Triggers, Views, Page Layouts, Workflows and generate different kinds of reports/dashboards according to requirements and cloud application solutions development on Force.com development platform.

Salesforce Lightning

The most efficient strategy for migrating to the Lightening experience comes with customizing each of the components. This is done to make sure that they adhere to AppExchange’s security guidelines for publishing.

Custom Salesforce Application Testing

With customized automation testing frameworks, functional testing is done on each salesforce component and workflow. This brings down the delivery cycles by a significant amount.
