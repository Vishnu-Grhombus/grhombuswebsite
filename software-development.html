The choice of technology plays a crucial role when it comes to developing an app or a website. Choosing the right technologies is a difficult task since many technologies exist for similar tasks, Pros and Cons of each technology must be analysed extensively to boil down to an optimum technical Stack for development. Choosing the technology also depends on your company’s needs and the advantages each of Java/.Net/Python can individually offer.

Java Enterprise Edition and the applications developed in it can run on any platform. Whereas, the applications developed in .NET, on the other hand, can work seamlessly on Windows Environment. Python’s dynamic system relies on brevity, fluidity, and experimentation in code, giving it more versatility than Java’s rigid style and it can be adapted into automated testing.
